AutoCAD: NCOPY

September 28, 2020 by George Godfrey

Credit: Adedapo Fasinro

NCOPY is used to copy a CAD entity into your drawing from a background xref. When used the copied entity  will come into your live drawing on the same layer it was in the original xref drawing.

To use this type NCOPY in your command box then select the entity / entities you want to copy after they have been selected press enter.

If you want the copied entity to lie exactly on top then just just continue to press enter however if you want the copied object to move elsewhere select your displacement point and click the area where you want to move it to.
